About Us

A Unique Identity

Illordia Hunts Paranormal is an investigative research team that seeks to bring a sense of "normalcy" to the world of the paranormal. While I.H.P may be new to the valley, its lead investigator and owner has been involved in the paranormal community since early 2001. We have taken part in a wide range of private investigations that help individuals come to terms with their experiences. Outside of our community service requests we also host private paranormal investigative events where we bring the investigation and equipment to you. We strive to build lasting relationships and make a positive impact with all of our clients experiencing strange phenomena. We are not acclaimed mediums nor consider ourselves a simple entertainment venue. We use technology, deductive reasoning, research, and logic in our investigative practices. Afterall, we are all seeking answers, why not do it together?
